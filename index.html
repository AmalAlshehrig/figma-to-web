Toy Story

Taking place in a world where toys come to
life when humans are not present, the plot of
Toy Story focuses on the relationship between an
old-fashioned pull-string cowboy doll named Woody
and a modern space cadet action figure,Buzz Lightyear,
as Woody develops jealousy towards Buzz when he becomes
their owner Andy's favorite toy.
